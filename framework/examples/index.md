The holy grail in task management is figuring out the right set of buckets.  You do this by starting at a reasonable place and experimenting.  The name of the bucket gives it meaning.  This matters when you have a thing and want to figure out which bucket you will use for that.

The examples give you a particular choice the author has made for himself.  You may use it as an example, or a starting point or may pick some subset that suits you and add your own set of buckets that fit your unique situation in life right now.
