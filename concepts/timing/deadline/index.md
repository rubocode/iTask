## Deadline

A deadline indicates the worst case scenarios of how late you can be in getting a thing done.  Often, this is a useful thing to know and you are not the one controlling it.

Whether you have a know deadline or not, you can attach a due date to a thing to help juggle it around.  You can plan to get it done way before the deadline if the opportunity presents itself.
